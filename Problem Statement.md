## Problem Statement  
Students often study daily but fail to **analyze their learning effectively**. They don’t clearly identify:  
* What they understood  
* Where they struggled  
* What actions to take next  
This leads to:  
* Poor consistency  
* Weak concepts  
* No improvement tracking  
👉 **Objective:** Design a **deterministic decision tree** that guides a student through a **daily reflection process**, ensuring structured thinking, problem identification, and continuous improvement.
